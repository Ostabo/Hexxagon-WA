About The Game
This project is developed using the Scala Play Framework as an example for the lecture Web Applications at the HTWG Konstanz. Hexxagon is a strategy board game played in a two player mode. The board consists of several hexagonal tiles that are arranged in another hexagonal grid.

The two players take turns at placing their stones on one of the tiles. After occupying one tile, all the stones in adjacent fields will convert to the same that was just placed.

The goal of the game is to capture as many fields as possible. The player with most stones on the board wins.
